Get help

Stackoverflow: Ask specific questions about ArangoDB and tag your question with "arangodb"

Google Group: For questions which do not fit into the Stackoverflow format

Github's issue tracker: Report bugs or suggest features

IRC: Sometimes the developers also lurk on IRC #arangodb

Email: If you want to talk to us in private write to hackers(at)arangodb.org. Please note that email is not supposed to be used for support requests

Keep up to date

Twitter: Tweet @arangodb if you have questions or follow us for new updates

Blog: For detailed updates look into our blog
